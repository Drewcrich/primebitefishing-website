How it works (overview)

Yellowfin is an offshore all-day rating (no Major/Minor windows) built from phase lift, illumination lift, and a capped ocean block (SST + chlorophyll + gradients). Research chooses the drivers; we calibrate thresholds and weights so the output is stable. Exact weights and caps are proprietary.
