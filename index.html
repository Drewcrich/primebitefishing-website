Transparency: How Our Bite Logic Works

PrimeBite documents the research and variables behind our bite ratings. All our algorithms are based on peer-reviewed, open-access research. For each species and the baseline profile we publish variables we use, citations with quotes, and what is research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights, equations, and worked examples are proprietary.

Solunar Windows (Baseline)

Baseline is classic solunar timing only (Major/Minor windows, dawn/dusk overlap, moon-illumination tweak). Best for freshwater when your species isn't listed (e.g. bass, crappie, bluegill). Not species-verified.

  • Solunar Windows (Baseline) — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables, citations, and evidence rationale. Exact calibration values are proprietary.

Yellowfin Tuna

Yellowfin is our reference offshore profile (phase + illumination + oceanography with anti-double-counting caps). We publish a dedicated research & methodology page (variables, citations, and evidence).

  • Yellowfin Tuna — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Mahi‑mahi

Mahi uses an offshore all‑day rating driven by moon phase (shared Yellowfin/Mahi curve), plus a small capped ocean block (SST + chlorophyll‑a). We publish a dedicated research & methodology page (variables, citations, and evidence).

  • Mahi‑mahi — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Big Eye Tuna

Our Big Eye Tuna rating system uses moon phase patterns and oceanographic data (sea surface temperature and chlorophyll-a) to predict activity.

  • Bigeye Tuna — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Black Marlin

Black Marlin (v1) is an offshore all‑day rating driven by moon phase (quarter‑phase peaks), a modest seasonality multiplier (GBR‑anchored, applied globally as a modeling choice), and an SST-only ocean modifier (capped; includes a conservative cold out‑of‑habitat penalty).

  • Black Marlin — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Walleye (Freshwater)

Walleye (v1) is a freshwater Major/Minor-window rating anchored to open-access evidence for moon position (overhead/underfoot), gibbous-over-quarter phase ordering, and crepuscular / low-light effects. The exact lift sizes and seasonality cap are calibrated to produce stable 1.0–5.0 ratings.

  • Walleye — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Muskie (Freshwater)

Muskie (v1) is a freshwater Majors-only window rating (moon overhead/underfoot) anchored to open-access evidence for new + waning gibbous phase ordering and strong dusk dominance. The exact lift sizes are calibrated, and Major-window ratings use percentile mapping so 1.0 and 5.0 are equally rare (commercial/UI scale mapping; raw-model math remains available).

  • Muskie — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Northern Pike (Freshwater)

Northern Pike (v1) is a freshwater all‑day rating (no Major/Minor “bite times”) anchored to open-access evidence that catch rates are higher around new + full moons, at higher wind speeds (within the observed range), and during dusk. The curve shapes and magnitudes are calibrated to produce a stable 1.0–5.0 score.

  • Northern Pike — Research & Methodology (Website) — Variables we use, citations with quotes, and what's research-backed vs calibrated. Exact weights and equations are proprietary.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

The PrimeBite Fishing app is designed to store your fishing data locally on your device. We do not run servers for this app, we do not track you, and we do not share your data.

What We Collect (On Your Device)

  • Catch Photos (optional): References (URIs) to photos you attach to entries.
  • Fishing Notes: Text you add to your logbook.
  • Location Data (optional): GPS coordinates when you choose to tag a catch.
  • Preferences: Settings like pounds vs. kilograms.
  • Notification Preferences (optional): Settings for forecast notifications, including species selection, rating thresholds, and notification times.
All information above is stored locally on your device. We do not collect it on our servers.

Why We Use This Data

  • Logbook: Save and review your catches.
  • Optional GPS Tagging: Remember where a catch happened.
  • Bite Windows: Show suggested times using sunrise/sunset and moon phase.
  • Preferences: Keep your chosen units and settings.

Permissions

  • Location (Foreground Only, Optional): Requested only when you choose to tag a catch location.
  • Photos/Library (Optional): Requested only when you choose to attach a photo.

The app works without granting these permissions.

Notifications

The app may send you push notifications for forecast reminders if you enable this feature. Notification preferences (including which species to monitor, rating thresholds, and notification times) are stored locally on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app.

Online Services We Call

Open-Meteo (Sunrise/Sunset and Weather Data): We query Open-Meteo for sunrise/sunset times and weather data (temperature, humidity, wind, precipitation, cloud cover, etc.) using your coordinates and date to improve accuracy. Requests are anonymous. If the network is unavailable, the app uses a local fallback calculation.

NOAA CoastWatch ERDDAP (Ocean Data): For offshore species forecasts, we query NOAA's CoastWatch ERDDAP service for sea surface temperature (SST) data using your coordinates and date. This is a free, public U.S. government service. Requests are anonymous. NOAA's privacy policy: https://www.noaa.gov/privacy-policy

Copernicus Marine Service (Chlorophyll Data): For offshore species forecasts, we query the Copernicus Marine Service (via a proxy server) for chlorophyll-a concentration data using your coordinates and date. Requests are anonymous. The proxy server does not store or log any user data. Copernicus privacy policy: https://marine.copernicus.eu/privacy-policy

Subscription Services

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases for our premium features (PrimeBite Pro). RevenueCat may collect:

  • Your Apple ID or Google account information (for purchase verification)
  • Purchase and subscription history
  • Device identifiers
  • Subscription status

This data is used solely to manage your subscription and restore purchases. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Premium Features: The app offers a premium subscription (PrimeBite Pro) that provides access to extended forecast features. Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store and are subject to their terms and conditions. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Data Sharing and Tracking

  • We do not share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Your Control

  • Export: Create a JSON backup of your logbook in Settings.
  • Import: Restore a JSON backup in Settings.
  • Delete Individual Entries: Remove any catch from your logbook.
  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app removes local data from your device.

Children

This app is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Your data remains on your device. Avoid sharing exported backups publicly.
